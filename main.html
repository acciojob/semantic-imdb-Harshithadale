The story follows Shivudu (Mahendra Baahubali), a young man raised in a village, who discovers his royal lineage and his connection to the kingdom of Mahishmati. As he learns about his father, Amarendra Baahubali, and the betrayal that led to his tragic fate, Shivudu seeks revenge against his evil uncle Bhallaladeva, who wrongfully seized the throne. The tale unfolds with grand battles, family conflicts, and Shivudu’s journey to reclaim his father’s honor and free his mother, Devasena, from captivity. The mystery of "Why did Kattappa kill Baahubali?" is finally revealed in the second film, leading to a thrilling climax.

Actors:

The Baahubali movies had a star-studded cast with powerful performances:
  • Prabhas played Baahubali (father & son), the brave and righteous hero.
  • Anushka Shetty as Devasena, a fierce warrior queen.
  • Rana Daggubati as Bhallaladeva, the ruthless villain.
  • Tamannaah Bhatia as Avanthika, a rebel fighter.
  • Sathyaraj as Kattappa, the loyal but conflicted warrior.
  • Ramya Krishnan as Sivagami, the strong and commanding queen.
  • Nassar as Bijjaladeva, the cunning and manipulative schemer.
